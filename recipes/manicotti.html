Manicotti

This manicotti recipe makes a comforting dinner your family will love. The kids like to help stuff the noodles too! Delicious served with a crispy salad and garlic bread.

Ingridients

  • Store bought or homemade manicotti pasta shells
  • Ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan cheeses.
  • Two eggs
  • Parsley, salt, and ground black pepper.

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the manicotti.
  2. Make the filling and spread sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  3. Stuff the shells and arrange them over the sauce.
  4. Pour the remaining sauce over the shells and sprinkle with Parmesan.
  5. Bake the manicotti until bubbly.
  6. In an oven preheated to 350 degrees F, this manicotti recipe should be perfectly cooked and bubbly after about 45 minutes.
