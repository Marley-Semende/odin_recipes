Maple Salmon


Description

Maple Salmon is an easy-to-make side dish that can be served with steamed rice. Made in minutes, this simple recipe is healthy and people of all age groups will love it.

Ingridients

  • 450 gm salmon fish
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. First of all, take a small bowl and add maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Now take a baking dish, place the salmon and coat it with the maple syrup mixture.
  3. After marinating the salmon, refrigerate it for 30 minutes.
  4. In the meantime, preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  5. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven, and bake the dish uncovered for 20 minutes, in between check with a fork.
  6. Serve with rice.
