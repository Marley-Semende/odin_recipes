Lasagna


Decription

Lasagna with Meat Sauce is a delightful Italian recipe that can be prepared for treating someone special. Loaded with cheese, this lasagna recipe might seem difficult at first with expert cooking skills. However, much to your surprise, the more hard work you will use for making this amazing dish, the more fruitful it will be. T

Ingridients

  • 1 kilograms ground mutton
  • 2 cups tomato ketchup
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 8 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 9 sliced & slit lasagna sheets
  • 3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 80 ml tomato puree
  • 2 pinches oregano

Steps

  1. Cook mutton and sausage over medium heat until they are soft and tender.
  2. Add tomato sauce, tomato puree, garlic cloves, sugar, salt, pepper, oregano and leave to simmer.
  3. In a bowl, combine the eggs, parsley, cottage cheese, ricotta and parmesan cheese. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in an ungreased baking dish.
  4. Make the layering using cheese, lasagne sheet and meat sauce. Layer with lasagne sheet, provolone cheese, 2 cups of cottage cheese mixture, 1 cup of mozzarella, lasagne sheet, 2 cups of meat sauce, remaining cottage cheese mixture and 1 cup of mozzarella. Top with remaining sheets, meat sauce and mozzarella.
  5. Bake the lasagna for 50 minutes at 375 degrees, cover and bake at 375 degrees Fahrenheit for around 50 minutes. Then uncover and bake for 20 minutes more. Let it stand for 15 minutes before cutting or it will disintegrate. Once it cools down, cut into squares and serve hot.
